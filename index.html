How to Play

You will be given 5 words, and each word contains the same three fixed interior letters.
Once you begin the game, you will receive a clue for your first word.
Using that clue, try to guess the first and last letter that, when combined with the fixed interior letters, will form a real word.
Incorrect guesses are not pentalized.
Guess all 5 words in under 2 minutes and you win!
New puzzle daily at midnight!
